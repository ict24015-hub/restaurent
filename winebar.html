French Wine & Champagne Bar

Le Bar à Vins

Our unique French wine & champagne bar, 'Le Bar à Vins' offers you a never before experience in Sri Lanka, with remarkable wines. They are sourced directly from producers of the vineyards in Bourgogne, Bordeaux, Cahors, Sancerre, Chablis and Champagne.

Whether you are a wine connoisseur or novice, this is an invitation to experiment world-class French wine paired with a 'fromage et charcuterie', the famous cheese and cold meats on a wooden platter.

The wine bar is open from 9 a.m. to 8:30 p.m at least.
