Luxury Accommodation At Le Grand Paris

Luxury rooms Hotel

Le Grand Paris de Colombo offers a unique blend of comfort and style in an intimate setting. Our rooms are thoughtfully designed to ensure you escape from the daily routine, and hustle and bustle of Colombo. These spacious rooms, with modern amenities, and personalized service are an ideal retreat for both leisure and business travelers.

This boutique hotel is designed for guests aged 12 and over, offering a sophisticated and peaceful experience.
